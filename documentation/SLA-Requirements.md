# SLA Requirements (Per SOP MK-03-02.01)

## Standard Complaints

### Initial Response
- **Timeframe:** 2 working days
- **Action:** Acknowledgment email sent to customer
- **Automated:** Yes (Flow 1)

### Full Resolution
- **Timeframe:** 5 working days
- **Action:** Complaint resolved and closed
- **Automated:** Reminders and escalations (Flow 3)

## Adverse Events (Critical Priority)

### Acknowledgment
- **Timeframe:** 1 hour
- **Action:** Immediate alert to MD, HR, Regulatory, Pharmacovigilance
- **Automated:** Yes (Flow 1)

### Assessment
- **Timeframe:** 4 hours
- **Action:** Initial assessment completed
- **Automated:** Alert only, manual assessment required

## Reminder Schedule

### Day 3 Reminder
- **Trigger:** Complaint open for 3+ days
- **Recipients:** Assigned department manager
- **Frequency:** Daily at 8:00 AM
- **Automated:** Yes (Flow 3)

### Day 5 Escalation
- **Trigger:** Complaint open for 5+ days
- **Recipients:** 
  - MD: md@tpg.co.zw
  - HR: hr@tpg.co.zw
  - Department Manager
- **Frequency:** Daily at 8:00 AM
- **Automated:** Yes (Flow 3)

## Status Definitions

| Status | Definition | SLA Impact |
|--------|------------|------------|
| Open | Newly received, not yet reviewed | Clock starts |
| Under Review | Being investigated | Clock running |
| Awaiting Response | Waiting for customer/supplier info | Clock paused (optional) |
| Resolved | Solution provided to customer | Clock stops |
| Closed | Customer confirmed satisfaction | Case complete |

## Working Days
- Monday to Friday
- Excludes public holidays
- Business hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

## Metrics to Track
1. Average time to acknowledgment
2. Average time to resolution
3. % resolved within 5 days
4. % requiring escalation
5. Customer satisfaction rate
6. Complaints by type
7. Complaints by department

## Reporting
- Weekly summary report (manual)
- Monthly trend analysis (manual)
- Dashboard view in SharePoint (recommended)
